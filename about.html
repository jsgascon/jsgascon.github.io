About

My interests are in tech, gaming, and computers. My interest in gaming began when I played Contra 3 on the super nintendo. My first experience with computers was when I got to play Commandos: Behind Enemy Lines on Windows 95. Video games have played a part in learning new things, espeically in computers. In middle school, I took a class consisting of history and english that included using a laptop. It was significant because I learned a lot from surfing the internet. I spend a lot of time on download.com where they had a lot of free software. I tried many software such as antivirus, cleaning utilities, game demos, and desktop enhancements. In high school, I played counter-strike: source and got to manage servers. I tried many mods/plugins that exposed me to some scripting and coding. I also researched and build my own computer. A lot my experience with tech was the result of helping and solving tech issues for others.
